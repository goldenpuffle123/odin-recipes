Souffle Pancakes

Description

This souffle pancakes recipe makes 2 servings or 6 pancakes. They're fluffly, light, and creamy, and perfectly paired with ice cream, fresh fruit, or whipped cream!

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs, split into whites and yolks
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1/4 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup of cake flour
  • 1/2 cup of whole milk

Steps

  1. Split the eggs into whites and yolks.
  2. Mix vanilla extract, 1/8 cup of sugar, cake flour, and milk into yolks.
  3. Using stand/handheld mixer, mix whites while gradually incorporating the rest of the sugar.
  4. Mix until stiff peaks.
  5. Fold yolk mixture into whites.
  6. Cook mixture 3 pancakes at a time on a non-stick pan. Put a couple drops of water and cover to steam.
  7. Finish, and add toppings to enjoy!
